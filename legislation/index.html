What's Inside

Everything a government affairs practice runs on.

Built with the money layer underneath, so every bill, vote, and stakeholder comes with financial context no other tracker has.

📜
Money-Aware Bill Tracking
Track any California or federal bill. Stage changes, hearings, amendments, and votes arrive as alerts. Every floor vote is enriched with each legislator's contribution history, so a vote count is also a money map.
⚖️
Contradiction Detection
BallotBase cross-references who's lobbying a bill against who's funding the members voting on it. When a legislator takes money from an organization lobbying a bill they voted on, it gets flagged, automatically, from public data.
🕸️
The Full Money Network
Expand any organization into its complete network: the lobbying firms it hires and what it pays them, the bills those firms work, the firm's other clients, and campaign contributions flowing to sitting legislators, across state and city disclosures.
📡
Registration Signals
Know who entered the fight before the fight is public. Alerts when a new lobbying registration matches your issues, when an organization retains a new firm, and when a cluster of registrations forms around a policy area within days of each other.
🎯
Tripwire: money early warning
Put a watch on a bill and BallotBase watches the money both ways. Inside money, when a donor you're tracking gives to a legislator whose vote you need. And outside money, when an independent expenditure starts spending to support or oppose the bill. Either way it fires the day it lands, across state, federal, and city filings, and names who is really behind the money, the bills they lobby, and the coalitions they fund, so you see the intent, not just a dollar amount.
🌱
Initiative Lifecycle
Manage policy from idea to outcome. Draft language with version history, build the coalition, track signature gathering, then convert the initiative into a tracked bill or ballot measure with the full history carried forward.
🗂️
Client-Ready Workflow
Issue portfolios, legislator tracking, contact tracking, and activity logs organized by client. AI-drafted client reports compile bill status, votes, money context, and your logged work into a deliverable. Compliance calendars keep quarterly deadlines visible.
