Features

The analysis that used to take days now takes minutes.

📍
Donor Geography
Map contributions by state, city, ZIP, and congressional district. In-state vs. out-of-state concentration at a glance.
💰
Fundraising Efficiency
Real cost-to-raise, burn rate, cash on hand, and self-funding ratio. Know which campaigns are healthy and which are running out of runway.
🔍
Donor Intelligence
Search millions of FEC Schedule A records by name, employer, occupation, or ZIP. Filter and export. Cross-reference donors across races.
🔔
Donation Alerts
Get notified when a significant contribution hits any committee you're tracking. Know when the money moves before anyone else does.
📡
IE Mapping
Track independent expenditure spending across a race. Who's spending, for or against which candidate, how much, and when.
📄
Client-Ready Briefings
Money Map outputs a formatted PDF with stat cards, donor tables, IE callouts, and contradiction analysis. Sourced, not inferred. Ready to send.
🤖
Contradiction Analysis
BallotBase compares candidate platform language against observed financial patterns and flags the gaps. Data vs. data, not opinion.
🗂️
Workspaces
Multi-client organization for agencies managing multiple races and candidates simultaneously.
📈
Historical Cycles
Compare fundraising performance across every cycle a committee has been active. See where momentum is building or fading.
