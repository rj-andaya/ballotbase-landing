The problem

Oregon's record is split across three systems that never talk

  • ✕ Campaign money lives in ORESTAR, lobbying in OGEC, bills and votes in OLIS, three separate systems
  • ✕ Nothing connects the money to the members who moved a bill
  • ✕ The earliest signal, a draft on an interim agenda, sits where no bill tracker looks
  • ✕ By the time a bill has a number, the window to weigh in is already closing
  • ✕ Testimony and positions are buried in individual PDF filings
The BallotBase solution

One view, from the first draft to the final vote

  • ✓ Bills, lobbying, and money joined together, on the issues you actually track
  • ✓ The pre-bill layer surfaced, Legislative Concept drafts before they become bills
  • ✓ Every hearing and posted agenda, sorted by when it appeared, not just when it meets
  • ✓ Support, oppose, and neutral, shown as the submitter's own OLIS label, never guessed
  • ✓ Refreshed on Oregon's own filing and legislative calendar
