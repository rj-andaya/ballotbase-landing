Industry groups register lobbyists, retain firms, and fund committees months before they surface publicly. By the time you see them at a hearing, the groundwork — and the checks — are already in.

What BallotBase Does

From concept to outcome, with the money visible the whole way.

The same intelligence platform lobbying firms use — pointed at your mission.

🌱
Initiative Lifecycle
A system of record for policy before it's a bill: draft language with version history, coalition building, sponsor search, signature tracking for ballot measures — then one-click conversion to a tracked bill or measure with everything carried forward.
🔎
Opposition Money, Mapped
See who funds the campaigns against your issue: the employers, PACs, and lobbying firms involved, what they spend, and which legislators receive it — across state filings and the city-level disclosures nobody else reads.
📡
Early-Warning Signals
Registration alerts tell you when new lobbying activity forms around your issue — a new employer registers, a firm gets retained, a cluster appears. Weeks of lead time instead of a surprise at the hearing.
🗺️
Geographic Issue Signals
Census-derived messaging signals and donor-capacity scores for every zip code show where your issue resonates and where supporters with capacity live — so field, fundraising, and comms aim at the same map.
📜
Learn From Every Similar Fight
Lookalike analysis finds past bills like yours — what passed, what died, who voted which way, and which funders showed up on each side. Your strategy starts from the historical record, not a hunch.
🤝
Coalition & Legislator Tracking
Track supporters, opponents, and undecided targets per bill. Every legislator comes with vote history and a fundraising profile, so outreach is prioritized by who's actually movable.
