What's covered

Everything the FEC reports — made readable.

BallotBase pulls directly from FEC filings and transforms them into clear, visual analytics.

🏛️
House & Senate Campaigns
Track any congressional candidate committee. Compare incumbents and challengers in the same district.
🏦
PACs & Super PACs
Analyze independent expenditure committees — what they're spending, who they're targeting, and who's funding them.
🗺️
Donor Geography
Map contributions by city, state, and congressional district. See in-district vs. out-of-district support at a glance.
📈
Fundraising Efficiency
Calculate cost-per-dollar raised across filing periods. Identify campaigns that are lean vs. burning through cash.
📊
Contribution Size Distribution
Break down small-dollar, mid-range, and large contributions. Understand the grassroots depth of any campaign.
🔍
Competitive Intelligence
Benchmark any committee against opponents in the same race. Spot fundraising gaps and momentum shifts early.
