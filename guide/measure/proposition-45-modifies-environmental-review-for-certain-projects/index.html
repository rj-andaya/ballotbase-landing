PROPOSITION 45 MODIFIES ENVIRONMENTAL REVIEW FOR CERTAIN PROJECTS

CA · 2026-11-03

In plain English

This ballot measure would change how environmental reviews work for certain projects. It would modify the rules that determine which projects need detailed environmental studies before they can move forward.

If this passes

What would actually change. No predictions, no opinions.

  • Some projects would be exempt from or have shorter environmental review processes
  • The specific projects affected would be those meeting criteria defined in the measure
  • Environmental review requirements would be modified rather than eliminated entirely
  • The measure applies to future projects that meet its conditions
