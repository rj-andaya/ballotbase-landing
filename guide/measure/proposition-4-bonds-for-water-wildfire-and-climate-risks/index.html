PROPOSITION 4 Bonds for Water, Wildfire, and Climate Risks

Passed CA · 2024-11-05

In plain English

This ballot measure would authorize the state to borrow money by issuing bonds. The borrowed money would be used for water infrastructure, wildfire prevention and response, and climate-related projects.

If this passes

What would actually change. No predictions, no opinions.

  • The state would be allowed to issue bonds (borrow money) for water, wildfire, and climate projects
  • Borrowed funds would be allocated to these three categories of projects
  • The state would need to repay the borrowed money over time
