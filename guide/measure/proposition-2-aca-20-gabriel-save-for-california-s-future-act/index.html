PROPOSITION 2 ACA 20 (Gabriel) Save for California's Future Act

CA · 2026-11-03

In plain English

This ballot measure would change the California Constitution to allow the state to set aside money in a special savings account during years when the state budget has extra funds. The state could use this saved money during years when the budget faces a shortfall.

If this passes

What would actually change. No predictions, no opinions.

  • The state could establish a 'rainy day fund' by depositing extra money into a special savings account when revenues exceed spending
  • The state could withdraw money from this fund during years when tax revenue falls short of spending needs
  • The measure would amend the California Constitution to allow this savings mechanism
