PROPOSITION 39 ESTABLISHES ADDITIONAL VOTER IDENTIFICATION AND CITIZENSHIP VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS

On the ballot CA · 2026-11-03

In plain English

This ballot measure would add new requirements for people to prove who they are and that they are citizens when they vote. It would set up additional ways to check voter identification and citizenship before someone can cast a ballot.

If this passes

What would actually change. No predictions, no opinions.

  • Voters would need to provide additional identification documents to prove they are citizens
  • Election officials would be required to verify citizenship status using additional verification methods
  • The specific identification and verification procedures would be established by this measure
