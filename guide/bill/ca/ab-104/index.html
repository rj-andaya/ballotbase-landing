AB 104, explained

Budget Act of 2025.

Passed into law Chaptered · Author: Gabriel

In plain English

This bill would make changes to how California spends state money for the 2025-26 fiscal year. It would add, remove, or adjust different spending amounts that were approved in the Budget Act of 2025. The bill would take effect right away if it passes.

If this passes

What would actually change, according to the bill's official digest. No predictions, no opinions.

  • Certain amounts of money set aside for state government would be increased, decreased, or removed
  • New spending items could be added to the state budget for 2025-26
  • The changes would go into effect immediately upon passage, rather than waiting for a later date
