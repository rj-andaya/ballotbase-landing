AB 650, explained

Planning and zoning: housing element: regional housing needs allocation.

Vetoed Vetoed · Author: Papan

In plain English

This bill requires the state's Department of Housing and Community Development to create a standard form or template for cities and counties to use when reporting what actions they have taken to promote fair housing. The department must finish creating this template by December 31, 2026.

If this passes

What would actually change, according to the bill's official digest. No predictions, no opinions.

  • The Department of Housing and Community Development would have to develop a standardized reporting format by December 31, 2026
  • Cities and counties would use this standard format when reporting their fair housing programs and actions
  • The standard format would apply to the 4th and subsequent revisions of local housing elements
