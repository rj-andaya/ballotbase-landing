SB 105, explained

Budget Acts of 2021, 2023, 2024, and 2025.

Passed into law Chaptered · Author: Wiener

In plain English

SB 105 would make changes to four budget laws from 2021, 2023, 2024, and 2025 that set aside money for state government. This bill would add, remove, or modify some of those spending amounts. It would take effect right away if it passes.

If this passes

What would actually change, according to the bill's official digest. No predictions, no opinions.

  • Some budget spending amounts from the 2021-22, 2023-24, 2024-25, and 2025-26 fiscal years would be added, changed, or removed
  • The bill would take effect immediately upon passage, rather than on a later date
  • The specific changes to individual spending items would depend on the details in the bill's amendments
